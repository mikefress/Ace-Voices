Why Choose Us

Full Service

We are a 100% online, remote working talent agency that does it all for you. We organise Zoom conference calls so all you need to do is tell us who will attend and when, and we’ll bring along our trusted sound engineer to mix and master your final track. From script to final delivery, we’ve got it covered for you without breaking a sweat.

We want to encourage and represent youthful diverse people from all backgrounds such as BAME, LGBTQI+ and all walks of life to share their stories for us to connect with and feel the emotion in their voices. We believe your brand or project will be elevated to new heights and get a better more authentic performance.

Providing value as the new modern wave

Why Join Us

We are a tight-knit community

We want to be a family of misfits, of those who felt like imposters trying to fit in but are really just waiting for their moment to shine with all the might of their fierce personality. We want voiceover artists to own their voices and help each other up to succeed as an Ace Talent. That’s why we are inclined to represent BAME, LGBTQI+, people with stories from different walks of life, and those who find it tough getting into any high barrier of entry industries that require university degrees or previous experience.

Ace Training – Sweat, baby, sweat!

We offer ongoing training and professional showreels from notablevoices.co.uk and we give you hints and tips every step of the way with regular support. We want to push our talent to reach for the stars with our bespoke training to make sure they’re at the right calibre for any project.

Let’s hustle – in our home castles!

We want people who are serious about being a professional voiceover artist. With hard work training your voice, it’s possible to be an Ace Talent as ongoing side hustle for those who have jobs elsewhere. Best part is that you can do voiceover work from the comfort of your home.

We are one and the same

Informal but hardworking

F.A.Q.

Question: When you say you’re a remote online full service voiceover talent agency, what does that mean exactly?

Answer: It means we are a talent agency representing voiceover talent exclusively without any physical offices to go to! We believe in 100% remote working online without the costs of rent hanging over us for us to recoup in our pricing to clients. As for full service, we want to do what it says on the tin to get the script turned into a final mixed track to go on any project. This includes providing the VO talent, organising the conference call to hear the recording and providing the sound engineer to mix and master the final track.

Question: When are your working hours?

Answer: Our working hours are 9am to 5pm Monday to Friday. After hour projects can be discussed.

Question: Where are you based?

Answer: We are based in London so any emails sent around the world will need to bear in mind that we are in the GMT timezone.

Question: So…you have no straight, white middle-aged talent whatsoever?

Answer: Not necessarily! We just want to focus more on everyone else but we’ll have one or two who believe in our cause and fulfil our criteria of being hardworking, open-minded and unique.

Question: When can we see your talent options, pricing, or ways of working?

Answer: As we’re a brand new agency, we need more time and resource into putting together a more comprehensive website with all the info! We thank you for your patience and in the meantime, we’ll be able to share all the details with you over a quick email with the latest info.

Question: How do I apply to be represented as a voiceover artist by Ace Voices?

Answer: Email us at hello@acevoices.com the following:
- Your name and age (you must be over 18 and eligible to work in the UK)
- Your life story, the ups and downs, and who you are in a couple of paragraphs.
- Two or three sound clips of your voice (you can talk about anything, the things you like and what you’re passionate about!) from your iPhone for at least 30 seconds each. Feel free to showcase your different tones and range.
- Link to your Instagram page.
