Why Choose Us

Full Service

We are a 100% online, remote working talent agency that does it all for you. We organise Zoom conference calls so all you need to do is tell us who will attend and when, and we’ll bring along our trusted sound engineer to mix and master your final track. From script to final delivery, we’ve got it covered for you without breaking a sweat.

Not just a voice, but a story

At Ace Voices, we LOVE being different and care about who we’re helping. We want to encourage and represent youthful diverse people from all backgrounds such as BAME, LGBTQI+ and all walks of life to share their stories for us to connect with and feel the emotion in their voices. We believe your brand or project will be elevated to new heights and get a better more authentic performance.

Providing value as the new modern wave

Remote working is the future. We want to operate like a global community with talent and clients from around the world and with our lean cost business model and simplified contracts; we can better serve our clients anywhere in the world with a quality service at a competitive price.

We listen with our minds & our hearts

We want to make this service work for you as you’re helping us work for those who need to be heard most. We’ll listen to your comments and will always hope to improve on it! As we’re a nimble little agency, we like to keep things informal to encourage real connections and relationships full of optimism and respect in our fresh new way of working.

Pricing is clear and simple

Our three handy tables of usage costs means you’ll always be able to get an answer back to your brand clients and higher-ups SHARPISH. We try to be as transparent as possible so you’ll be able to focus purely on our talent.
